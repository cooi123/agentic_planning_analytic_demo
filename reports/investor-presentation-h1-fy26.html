Outlook — H2 FY26 Priorities

07

Kalgara Operational Priorities

  • Maintain gold production trajectory toward upper end of 130–155koz guidance
  • Continue North Pit Stage 2 waste stripping (baseload from FY28)
  • Complete open pit fleet rebuild program (8 trucks); deploy 2 new haul trucks (Q2 FY27)
  • TSF6 Stage 2 lift construction completion (on track)
  • H2 processing costs expected to be modestly lower than H1 (no major shutdown)

Creston Development Priorities

  • EPA approval targeted H2 FY26 — key prerequisite for FID
  • Continue decline tunnelling and early works investment on critical path
  • Contractor selection and EPCM engagement ahead of FID
  • Financial close of A$150m debt facility prior to or concurrent with FID

Resource Development Priorities

  • LKU maiden Mineral Resource Estimate — targeted Q3 FY26
  • Stage 3 Extension drilling — multi-year open pit mine life extension
  • Near-mine satellite deposit assessment (4 targets within 30km)
