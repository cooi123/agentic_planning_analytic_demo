Highlights

  • Operations: Record quarterly gold production of 44,273oz Au and 1,763t Cu at an AISC of A$2,296/oz Au — the lowest quarterly AISC in the Company's history. Gold recovery of 87.5% maintained.
  • Safety: One lost time injury occurred during the quarter, resulting in a 12-month moving average LTIFR of 0.3. Total Recordable Injury Frequency Rate (TRIFR) improved to 4.4.
  • Financial: Sales of 38,400oz Au at an average realised price of A$6,208/oz and 1,642t Cu at A$14,280/t, generating net revenue of A$246.8 million. Cash flow from operations of A$178.3 million with a cash build of A$112.6 million, closing at A$421.8 million, debt free.
  • FY26 Guidance: Based on H1 performance of 83,417oz at A$2,341/oz AISC, full-year production is expected to trend towards the upper end of guidance (130,000–155,000oz) and AISC towards the lower end (A$2,200–A$2,600/oz).
  • Growth — Creston FS: Creston Feasibility Study results announced 3 October 2025. Post-tax NPV5% of A$1.42 billion, IRR of 24.2% and 12-year mine life confirmed. Creston is Australia's fifth-largest undeveloped underground gold reserve.
  • Creston Development: A$18.2 million invested in Creston early works during the quarter. Decline tunnelling advanced a further 372m (cumulative 712m). FID targeted H1 FY27, subject to environmental approvals (EPA outcome targeted H2 FY26).
  • Resource Development: 54,204 metres of resource development drilling completed in the quarter from 10 active rigs. Lower Kalgara Underground project delivered the highest grade intercept to date: 48.6m @ 6.2g/t Au & 0.38% Cu from 412m (drill hole LKU4450092).

"The December quarter was a landmark period for Ridgecrest, delivering a record 44,273 ounces of gold at an AISC of A$2,296 — our lowest ever — while generating A$178 million in operating cash flow and building our cash balance to A$422 million. At the same time, we achieved a transformational milestone with the Creston Feasibility Study confirming a world-class underground gold-copper project with a post-tax IRR of 24% and NPV of A$1.4 billion. Creston's development is expected to be fully funded from our cash reserves together with the A$150 million committed debt facility, and we look forward to taking a Final Investment Decision in the first half of FY27."

— Thomas Hargreaves, Managing Director, Ironfield Resources Limited

Mining

Open Pit

Open pit ore mined during the December quarter was from West Pit Stage 4 and North Pit Stage 1 (refer Figure 1), totalling 2.09Mt at 0.62g/t Au and 0.08% Cu. Dump leach ore mined was 0.38Mt.

Total material mined (TMM) was 6.18Mt, including approximately 3.71Mt of waste — the fourth consecutive quarter of TMM growth, reflecting a 17% increase over the September quarter. North Pit Stage 2 waste stripping continued, with 3.4Mt of waste mined cumulatively to 31 December 2025. The overall North Pit Stage 2 design strip ratio of approximately 1.4 positions it as baseload ore feed from FY28.

The open pit fleet renewal program delivered the first three of a planned eight Caterpillar 789 dump truck rebuilds during the quarter. Orders for two additional Liebherr T 284 haul trucks were placed and are expected to deliver in Q2 FY27, further supporting open pit productivity growth.

Improved grade control via RC drilling continued to be rolled out across West Pit Stage 4. Open pit grades reconciled in line with expectations for the second consecutive quarter.

Underground

Ore mined from the Lower Kalgara Underground (LKU) was predominantly from C-Lode, Eastern Corridor and the newly developed Kylo target, totalling 0.26Mt at 1.74g/t Au and 0.62% Cu. Underground development metres totalled 1,382m for the quarter, with 792m of growth capital development extending the C-Lode system north and targeting the high-grade Kylo extension.

Processing

The December quarter delivered processed tonnes of 2.48Mt with average head grade of 0.64g/t Au and 0.09% Cu, the highest quarterly throughput and head grade in Ridgecrest's operating history. Gold recovery of 87.5% and copper recovery of 79.2% were maintained. Gold production of 44,273oz represented a 13% increase on the September quarter.

A scheduled 36-hour maintenance shutdown was completed in November 2025. Processing of West Pit Stage 4 material, which has a higher clay content requiring increased reagent usage, was partially offset by improved mill throughput. Mill availability was 92.4% for the quarter.

The TSF6 Stage 2 lift construction commenced during the quarter and is tracking on schedule, providing tailings capacity through to Q2 FY28. TSF6 Stage 3 is planned to commence in FY27.

Resource Development

A total of 54,204 metres of resource development drilling was completed during the December quarter from 10 active drill rigs, consistent with the September quarter (53,543 metres). The FY26 annual program of 220,000 metres remains on track, with 107,747 metres completed in H1 FY26.

Lower Kalgara Underground (LKU)

LKU resource growth drilling delivered the highest grade intercepts to date during the December quarter, with exceptional results including:

  • 48.6m @ 6.2g/t Au & 0.38% Cu (302gm Au) from 412m (LKU4450092)
  • 24.2m @ 8.8g/t Au & 0.14% Cu (213gm Au) from 468m (LKU4450092)
  • 42.4m @ 2.6g/t Au & 0.34% Cu (110gm Au) from 284m (LKU4450094)
  • 38.8m @ 3.1g/t Au & 0.28% Cu (120gm Au) from 318m (LKU4450086)

A maiden Mineral Resource Estimate for the LKU western extension is targeted in the March 2026 quarter. A third drill rig has been allocated to the LKU for the remainder of FY26.

West Pit Open Pit Extension

Stage 4 Extension drilling was completed during the period, with drill capacity shifting to the southern Stage 3 Extension which remains a priority target for Q3 FY26. Results support multi-year extension of open pit mine life beyond FY27.

Creston Underground Project

Feasibility Study Results

The Creston Feasibility Study (FS) was completed and announced on 3 October 2025, confirming the pathway to a world-class, long-life, lowest quartile cost Australian gold-copper mine leveraging existing Kalgara infrastructure.

Key highlights of the FS:

  • Undiscounted free cash flow of A$3.8 billion pre-tax and A$2.6 billion post-tax at base case metal prices (A$4,500/oz long-term gold)
  • NPV5% of A$2.0 billion pre-tax and A$1.42 billion post-tax at base case pricing; NPV5% increases to A$3.4 billion pre-tax and A$2.4 billion post-tax at spot gold (A$6,250/oz)
  • IRR post-tax of 24.2% at A$4,500/oz gold (34.8% at A$6,250/oz)
  • Life-of-Mine Plan: 12.8Mt mined at 3.1g/t Au & 0.28% Cu for 1.27Moz gold and 36kt copper contained
  • First gold expected ~2.5 years from Final Investment Decision (FID), targeted H1 FY27
  • Pre-production capital expenditure of A$420 million (including 10% contingency and 3% growth allowance)
  • Steady-state production of 120,000oz Au and 4kt Cu per annum at AISC of A$1,580/oz
  • Initial mine life of 12 years; ore reserve of 12.8Mt at 3.1g/t Au & 0.28% Cu (1.27Moz Au, 36kt Cu)
  • Creston is Australia's fifth-largest undeveloped underground gold reserve

Permitting & Approvals

EPA assessment of the Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) submitted in September 2025 is ongoing. EPA approval is targeted in H2 FY26 and is required prior to FID. Commonwealth EPBC Act assessment is progressing in parallel.

Early Works

Decline tunnelling advanced 372m during the December quarter, for a cumulative 712m. Reinforced concrete arch sections and footings delivery commenced, with portal installation ongoing. Preliminary works for blind bores progressed with drill cutter head fabrication completed. A$18.2 million of early works were invested during the quarter, funding critical path activities ahead of FID.

Corporate

Capital Adequacy & Financing

Ridgecrest closed the December quarter with A$421.8 million in cash, debt free. The A$150 million committed corporate debt facility (ANZ, CBA, Westpac) entered into in September 2025 remains available but undrawn, providing additional liquidity for Creston construction alongside Ridgecrest's substantial cash reserves. The A$30 million working capital facility (ANZ/CBA) remains undrawn and available to 30 June 2026.

Ridgecrest expects Creston's development to be fully funded from cash together with the A$150 million binding debt commitment, without the need for equity issuance.

About Ironfield Resources

Ironfield Resources Limited (ASX: RMC) is an Australian gold and copper producer operating the Kalgara Gold-Copper Mine in the Murchison Province of Western Australia. Kalgara is a fly-in fly-out mine with open pit and underground operations, a 10Mtpa processing plant producing gold doré and copper-gold concentrate, and an established workforce and infrastructure base. Ridgecrest also owns 100% of the Creston Underground Project, a high-grade gold-copper development 25km south of Kalgara, underpinned by a Feasibility Study confirming post-tax NPV5% of A$1.42 billion and IRR of 24.2%.
