Mining

Open Pit

Open pit ore mined during the September quarter was from West Pit Stage 4 and North Pit Stage 1 (refer Figure 1), totalling 1.78Mt at 0.59g/t Au and 0.08% Cu. Dump leach ore mined was 0.26Mt.

Total material mined (TMM) was 5.24Mt including approximately 3.18Mt of waste. Continued focus on pit productivity improvements in West Pit Stage 4 drove an 18% quarter-on-quarter increase in mill feed ore mined. North Pit Stage 2 waste stripping, which commenced during the quarter, will provide baseload feed from FY28.

Grade control drilling using reverse circulation (RC) methods has been progressively incorporated across West Pit Stage 4, replacing reliance on blast-hole sampling alone. This is expected to improve ore definition resolution and reconciliation confidence in coming quarters.

Underground

Ore mined from the Lower Kalgara Underground (LKU) during the quarter was predominantly from the C-Lode and Eastern Corridor zones, totalling 0.21Mt at 1.82g/t Au and 0.58% Cu. Underground development metres totalled 1,246m for the quarter, including 688m of growth capital development targeting the C-Lode Extension and Eastern Corridor North.

Processing

The September quarter delivered processed tonnes of 2.34Mt with average head grade of 0.60g/t Au and 0.08% Cu. Gold recovery of 87.0% was maintained, consistent with the strong performance of recent quarters. Gold production of 39,144oz and copper production of 1,274t were achieved.

A scheduled 48-hour mill maintenance shutdown was completed in August 2025. Mill availability for the quarter was 91.2%, in line with plan. The tailings storage facility (TSF6) Stage 2 lift is expected to commence construction in the December quarter, providing capacity through to Q2 FY28.

Creston Underground Project

The Creston Underground Project is Ridgecrest's high-grade gold-copper development project located 25km south of the Kalgara processing plant, connected via an established haul road. The project is expected to leverage existing Kalgara infrastructure, significantly reducing capital intensity and time to first production.

Feasibility Study

The Creston Feasibility Study (FS) was substantially complete at the end of the September quarter, with final results targeted for announcement in October 2025. The FS has confirmed the project as a high-return, long-life underground gold-copper mine. Preliminary FS outcomes indicate:

  • Ore Reserve of 12.8Mt at 3.1g/t Au & 0.28% Cu (1.27Moz Au, 36kt Cu contained)
  • Steady-state annual production of approximately 120,000oz Au and 4kt Cu
  • Initial mine life of 12 years total life of mine
  • Pre-production capex of approximately A$420 million
  • Post-tax NPV5% of approximately A$1.4 billion at A$4,500/oz gold
  • Post-tax IRR of approximately 24% at A$4,500/oz gold

Note: FS outcome figures above are preliminary estimates pending final announcement. Refer to the October 2025 ASX announcement for final confirmed metrics and associated cautionary statements regarding production targets.

Permitting & Approvals

The Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) was formally submitted to the Western Australian Environmental Protection Authority (EPA) during the September quarter, a key milestone in the approvals process. EPA approval is required prior to a Final Investment Decision (FID), with receipt targeted in H2 FY26.

Early Works

Creston decline access tunnelling advanced 340m during the quarter. Boxcut civil works and portal establishment were completed and reinforced concrete arch sections are being prepared for installation. A$7.6 million of early works were invested during the quarter to advance critical path activities ahead of FID.

Corporate

Debt Facility

  • A$75 million Revolving Credit Facility (Facility A)
  • A$75 million Revolving Credit Facility (Facility B)

About Demo Company A

Demo Company A Limited (ASX: RMC) is an Australian gold and copper producer operating the Kalgara Gold-Copper Mine in the Murchison Province of Western Australia. Kalgara is a fly-in fly-out operation with open pit and underground mining, a 10Mtpa processing plant producing gold doré and copper-gold concentrate, and an established workforce and infrastructure base. Ridgecrest also owns 100% of the Creston Underground Project, a high-grade gold-copper development 25km south of Kalgara.
