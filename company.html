The Story

Dec 2024
Kalgara Acquisition
Demo Company A acquires the Kalgara Gold-Copper Mine — a 10Mtpa operation with open pit and underground mining in WA's Murchison Province. H1 FY25 reflects only one month of ownership.
H1 FY26
Record Performance
Kalgara delivers 83,417oz Au at AISC A$2,341/oz — record H1 production, lowest-ever quarterly AISC in Dec Q (A$2,296/oz), A$318m operating cash flow. Cash position more than doubles.
Oct 2025
Creston FS Confirmed
Feasibility Study: post-tax NPV₅% A$1.42bn, IRR 24.2%, 12-year mine life, A$420m pre-production capex, steady-state 120koz Au/yr at AISC A$1,580/oz. Fully funded — no equity required.
H1 FY27 Target
Final Investment Decision
EPA approval targeted H2 FY26 (prerequisite). Decline at 712m cumulative. FID then unlocks construction — first gold ~2.5 years from FID. Combined group output: 250–275koz/yr at lower unit costs.
